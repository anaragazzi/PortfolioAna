UI Design Project for YouCode

During the time I was working as a UI Designer at Chleba I had the opportunity to create a User Interface Design for YouCode, one of their main products. I was responsible for creating their mobile app as well as their landing page Interface. Here I will share some of the process of this project.

What is YouCode?

YouCode is a social network for professionals. It allows people to share contact information using QR code instead of printed business cards. For its MVP (Minimum Viable Product) I designed the mobile app and the landing page.
Since the business area is the focus of the product the app needed to be simple, fast, and easy to use. YouCode didn't had competitors in its initial phase. Therefore I had to find references in popular apps used by businesspeople, such as financial, job search, and social media apps.

My role

The product already had a defined logo, however I had the liberty of choosing the typographies as well as the color pallet. I also designed a complete icon library which would help the user journey through the product.
Since I was trying to reach a clean and intuitive app, I decided that I would use a sans serif font (Source Sans Pro). Along with that I designed a bottom navigation bar to make it easier to find basic functions, such as codes, my codes, contacts, and contacts solicitations.
