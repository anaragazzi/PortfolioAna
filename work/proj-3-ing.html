Below you can find some printed advertising works that I made when I was working in Biwa Brasil, between the years of 2011 and 2012.

The work is mainly retouched and manipulated images. I took pictures off the jewels in a studio, and then I selected images from a online stock image to combine them.

I love to retouch and to manipulate images. They take a lot of work, but I am always happy with the result. I had the opportunity of work with this again in Cappuccino Digital. You can see some of these works in the Social Media section.
